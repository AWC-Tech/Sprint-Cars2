Sprint cars are high-powered, lightweight race cars built for dirt oval tracks.
Known for their massive wings and incredible speed, they are one of the most extreme forms of motorsport.

How To Drive A Sprint Car

  1. Start: Cars are push-started — no clutch or starter motor.
  2. Throttle: Smooth control is key to avoid spinning out.
  3. Sliding: Use controlled slides (drifting) into and out of corners.
  4. Strategy: Pull slide jobs to pass opponents on tight tracks.
