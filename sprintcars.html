Overview


Sprint Cars are used in a form of racing called "Sprint Car Racing", this is a very popular racing sport, where typically 24 drivers are on the track. Sprint Cars are very high powered motor vehicles, the weigh approximately 1,400 pounds, and output over 900 horsepower. They differ in a few different types, with some having a top, and other not. These tops are called wings, they are costmetic and also affects saftey and wind drag. This seperates the cars into two different categories, Winged sprint cards, and wingless sprint cars.
