Sprint Cars


Are you into Sprint Cars? Well, if you are, you have come to the right place.



Overview


Sprint Cars are used in a form of racing called "Sprint Car Racing". This is a very popular racing sport, where typically 24 drivers are on the track. Sprint Cars are very high-powered motor vehicles; they weigh approximately 1,400 pounds and output over 900 horsepower.
They differ in a few different types, with some having a top and others not. These tops are called wings; they are cosmetic and also affect safety and wind drag.
This separates the cars into two different categories: Winged sprint cars and wingless sprint cars.
